About the Event

The 3rd symposium hosted by the School of Information

and Communication Technology (SICT) promises an immersive

exploration of the cutting-edge advancements shaping the future

of food production. This symposium serves as a platform for scholars,

researchers, industry experts, and enthusiasts alike to converge

and delve into the transformative impact of emerging technologies

on agriculture.

At this symposium, attendees will have the opportunity to gain

invaluable insights into how Artificial Intelligence (AI), robotics,

and vertical farming are revolutionizing traditional agricultural

practices. From precision farming techniques powered by

AI algorithms to autonomous robotic systems streamlining

labor-intensive tasks, participants will witness firsthand how

technological innovation is reshaping the agricultural landscape.
